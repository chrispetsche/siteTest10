About me

I'm Chris and my passions include...

I am very much the type of person that can, and often does, become fascinated with everything that comes into my world.

With that, and as an extremely imaginative individual, I find it quite difficult to settle on any one thing, like favorites. Bias thoughts such as these tend to hinder the creative process, and deprives one of all the extraordinarily beautiful experiences that come with loving all diversities throughout life and beyond. And experiences are in my opinion one of the most valuable assets we all have. Each one, if viewed from the right perspective, is priceless due to the impact it can have as it ripples out into the world.

So, with my ever growing knowledge and focus as an artist, designer and developer, my personal life goal is to create and develop as many experiences as possible, of all sorts and types, to help change the world one perspective at a time!!!

My skills

My skills, tools & experiences.
I'm ALWAYS learning, so the tables are always growing. If you're looking for something specific, please use the page buttons to flip the tables at your own pace.

The style type buttons that follow toggle between tables of skills/ tool that apply to either art, design or development. Each are given a rough percentage based on 4 things:

- Number of years experience with skill or tool
- Number of projects skill or tool was used in
- How adaptable the skill or tool has become for me
- How close the skill or tool is to becoming second nature

For example, I’ve used the Unity game engine for nearly 10 years now. I’ve used the engine for a few dozen projects and used it to create a wide variety of projects for several different purposes. I usually turn to Unity for any type of technical work, games and much, much more! And most things I either know how to do or find the answer I need to make almost any of my ideas come to life.

Now, add in the fact that the engine is constantly being updated and, like everything else, there is always more to learn. So, I estimate my knowledge and ability with Unity to more or less hover around 80%.
